Replace `<>` with the host [for your region](https://docs.logz.io/docs/user-guide/admin/hosting-regions/account-region/#available-regions). For example, `listener.logz.io` if your account is hosted on AWS US East, or `listener-nl.logz.io` if hosted on Azure West Europe. The required ports are **rsyslog over TLS** = `5001` or **rsyslog** = `5000`
